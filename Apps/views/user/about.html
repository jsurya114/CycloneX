Our Story

Founded in 2020, CycloneX emerged from a simple dream: to create bicycles that are more than just transportation. We believe cycling is an art form, a lifestyle, and a powerful way to connect with the world around us.

Our journey began in a small workshop, driven by cyclists who were tired of compromise. We wanted bikes that didn't just move, but inspired movement – bikes that combine cutting-edge technology, sustainable design, and unparalleled performance.

Our Mission

To revolutionize cycling by creating high-performance, sustainable bicycles that empower riders to explore, challenge, and enjoy their journey.

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly materials and production processes that minimize environmental impact.

Innovation

Constantly pushing the boundaries of bicycle design and technology.

Community

Building a global community of passionate cyclists who inspire and support each other.
